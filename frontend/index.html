Angel Eyes supports two ways to monitor your baby with the same powerful AI detection. Choose the method that works best for your situation.

Mobile Camera

✅ Full app features (Routines, Community)

🎥

Logitech C270 Webcam

Use your USB webcam through a web browser for stable, long-duration monitoring.

✅ No battery drain
✅ Stable fixed position
✅ HD 720p video quality
✅ Quick browser setup (30 sec)
❌ No app features (monitoring only)
